ABOUT

The Sports Complex is an important unit of SIT Bhubaneswar. The main aim of the Council is to Initiate, Coordinate, Execute and Manage various kinds of Sports activities including Football, Basketball, Cricket, Badminton, Volleyball, Kabaddi and other such programmes to promote general interest in Games and Sports among the students of the college. This improves the standard of Competitive Sports and Games in the college.

Min objectives of the Sports Complex is to

  • Coordinate sports activities among different colleges
  • Promote interest among the budding youth in excelling in games and sports
  • Select and train sportsmen & women in different games
  • Depute teams for participation in different Inter College Tournament organized within the state
